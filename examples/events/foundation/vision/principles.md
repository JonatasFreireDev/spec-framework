Principles need human approval before they are used to reject or prioritize work.

## Purpose

Product principles guide trade-offs when agents or humans must choose between valid options.

## Candidate Principles

1. Product decisions must remain traceable to an approved problem and strategy.
2. User-facing flows should be specified before implementation planning begins.
3. Delivery Level and Priority must be explicit before tasks are generated.
4. Risks in privacy, permission, safety, or trust require approval before downstream work.

## Usage

When a feature, specification, design, or task conflicts with a principle, the responsible skill should stop, record the conflict, and request approval or revision.
