## Release Strategy

## Compatibility Migration And Backfill

| Change | Compatibility window | Migration/backfill | Validation | Cleanup trigger |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Attendance and audit schema. | Deploy additive nullable fields/store before validation writer. | No historical check-in or audit synthesis. | Existing event participation regression and concurrent validation test. | All supported consumers tolerate schema. |
| Permission integration and validation API. | Session query available before scanner flag. | No role changes generated by migration. | Authorized/denied role matrix. | Canonical role policy and old path retirement approved. |

## Monitoring And Decision Points

| Signal | Expected range | Pause/rollback threshold | Decision owner | Observation window |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Authorization/privacy/integrity | No unauthorized write, identity leak, or duplicate attendance. | Any confirmed violation. | Security and Product. | Continuous pilot. |
| Scanner/validation reliability | Establish approved internal baseline. | Sustained failure/latency or camera recovery gap harming venue operations. | Operations and Product. | Per event and five-minute windows. |

## Rollback And Recovery

| Failure mode | Rollback action | Data recovery | Maximum tolerated impact | Verification |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Authorization/privacy/data defect. | Disable organizer flag and validation writer; preserve evidence. | Review affected attendance under approved incident process; no automatic destructive rewrite. | No continued mutation/exposure after disable. | Endpoint/flag denial and audit review. |
| Venue reliability or camera failure. | Stop pilot and show unavailable guidance; do not enable unapproved manual search. | Retain valid attendance and invalidate active tokens if required. | Selected pilot event. | Signal recovery and support confirmation. |
