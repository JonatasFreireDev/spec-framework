# Journeys: Participate In Event

## Status

Draft.

## Primary Journey

1. Attendee decides to participate in an event.
2. Attendee confirms eligibility or RSVP status.
3. Attendee arrives at the venue during the check-in window.
4. Attendee presents event-scoped proof of participation.
5. Organizer validates the proof.
6. System records attendance and shows a success state.
7. Attendance becomes available for event operations and analytics.

## Friction Points

- Attendee cannot find their QR code.
- QR code is expired or belongs to the wrong event.
- Organizer has weak network connectivity.
- Organizer lacks permission for the event.
- Duplicate scans create confusion if idempotency is not clear.

## Success Signals

- Attendee can complete check-in without exposing unnecessary personal data.
- Organizer can validate quickly and confidently.
- Duplicate or invalid scans are handled safely.

## Responsible Skill

Primary owner: Journey AI.
