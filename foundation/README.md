# Foundation

## Purpose

The foundation folder explains why the product exists before domains, goals, features, or implementation work begin. It anchors product decisions in the problem, vision, and strategy.

## When To Use

Use this folder at product start, during strategic resets, or when a domain or feature has lost traceability to a validated product direction. Do not use it to document implementation details or feature-level behavior.

## Expected Files

- `problem/problem.md`: the core user or market problem.
- `problem/opportunities.md`: opportunities and assumptions derived from the problem.
- `problem/researches/`: research notes and evidence.
- `problem/interviews/`: interview summaries and quotes.
- `problem/context.md`: context, decisions, dependencies, and next step for problem discovery.
- `vision/vision.md`: product vision and target outcome.
- `vision/principles.md`: decision principles that guide trade-offs.
- `vision/north-star.md`: north-star metric or outcome.
- `vision/context.md`: context and approval state for the vision layer.
- `strategy/strategy.md`: product strategy and sequencing.
- `strategy/personas.md`: priority users and their jobs.
- `strategy/competitors.md`: alternatives and positioning.
- `strategy/metrics.md`: success, guardrail, and quality metrics.
- `strategy/roadmap.md`: roadmap framing by Delivery Level and Priority.
- `strategy/context.md`: context and handoff into domains.

## Responsible Skill

Primary owner: Product Orchestrator.

Specialist owners: Problem Discovery AI, Vision AI, Strategy AI, and Domain Architect AI.
