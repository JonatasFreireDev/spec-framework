This document captures possible product opportunities that emerge from the problem discovery layer. Each opportunity should remain traceable to evidence, assumptions, and a target user segment.

## Evaluation Criteria

- Clear target user.
- Repeated pain or meaningful unmet need.
- Product relevance to the intended vision.
- Feasible path to a walking skeleton.
- Risks and non-goals are explicit.
