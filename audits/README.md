# Audits

Audits verify quality, consistency, readiness, and risk across product artifacts. They should report findings before creating or changing canonical product scope.

## Available Gates

- `readiness/` - verifies whether an artifact can move to the next Product Engineering Framework step.

## Reports

Audit reports should include:

- scope;
- date;
- auditor skill or orchestrator;
- verdict;
- evidence;
- blocking findings;
- required fixes;
- residual risk.

## Rule

Audit findings are not product decisions. Findings become changes only after approval and, when relevant, a decision record.
